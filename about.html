About

A brief history of about us

Established in 2015, our company stands as a trusted supplier specializing in advanced drilling rigs and air compressors. Our versatile product lineup is meticulously engineered to adapt seamlessly to diverse operational scenarios, from rugged open-pit mines and underground tunnels to urban construction sites and offshore platforms. Whether tackling hard-rock excavation, deep-well drilling, or high-demand industrial air supply, our drilling rigs deliver precision and efficiency, while our air compressors ensure reliable, energy-saving performance. With a decade of expertise, we combine quality craftsmanship, innovative technology, and tailored solutions to meet the unique challenges of every project. Partner with us to experience equipment designed for unmatched durability and operational flexibility across any environment.

A new generation of drilling rigs and air compressors

Drilling Rigs

Automation and Intelligence: New - gen drilling rigs leverage sensors and AI to auto - optimize parameters, adapt to geology in real - time, and boost accuracy. This cuts human error, enhancing both efficiency and safety. Remote Monitoring: Integrated telemetry lets operators oversee rigs remotely via sensor data. Early problem detection and informed decisions minimize downtime and maximize productivity. Air Compressors

Reliability: Built from quality materials, new - gen compressors endure harsh conditions, last longer, and require less maintenance.
