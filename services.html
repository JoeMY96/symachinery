Drilling Rigs

Air-hydraulic combined DTH drill​
Integrating air and hydraulic systems, this drill uses air to power the DTH hammer for rock breaking and hydraulics for feed, rotation, and positioning. It offers high impact energy, precise control, and boosts productivity in mining and construction.​
Track-mounted DTH drill​
With crawler tracks, it moves easily over rough terrain, ideal for open-pit mining and large sites. The tracks ensure stability, quick setup, and better drilling accuracy.​
Track-mounted surface DTH drill​
Built for surface drilling, it combines track mobility with specialized features. Capable of large-diameter and deep holes, it’s flexible for foundation work, road cutting, and blasting prep.​
All-hydraulic surface DTH drill​
Fully hydraulic-driven, it provides smooth operation, consistent torque, and feed force. It adapts to different rock hardness, cuts component wear, and suits high-precision surface drilling.​
Modular track-mounted surface DTH drill​
Its modular design enables easy customization and maintenance. Components can be swiftly assembled or replaced, reducing downtime and offering cost-effective upgrades for varied drilling needs.​
Air compressor-integrated DTH drill rig​
This rig integrates a DTH system with an onboard air compressor, ditching external air supply. It simplifies setup, cuts transport costs, and ensures stable air supply for consistent performance in remote areas.

Air Compressors

Screw air compressor
Screw air compressors use interlocking helical rotors to compress air, delivering continuous, high-volume output with low pulsation. Highly efficient and reliable, they suit diverse industrial applications.​
Permanent magnet variable frequency air compressor​
Fitted with permanent magnet motors and variable frequency drives, these compressors adjust speed based on air demand. This cuts energy waste by up to 35%, ensuring stable pressure and lower costs for fluctuating air consumption scenarios.​
Mobile screw air compressor​
Mounted on trailers or trucks, mobile screw air compressors combine compression efficiency with mobility. They can quickly reach remote sites, providing on-demand air supply for construction and emergency repairs.​
Oil - free air compressor​
By eliminating oil lubrication, oil - free air compressors produce clean, contaminant - free air. Vital for industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and electronics, where oil contamination risks product quality.​
Piston air compressor​
Reciprocating pistons power piston air compressors, known for simplicity and affordability. Capable of high - pressure output, they're ideal for small - scale uses such as auto repair shops and home workshops.​
Variable frequency screw air compressor​
Merging screw compression with variable frequency tech, these compressors regulate motor speed according to air needs. Dynamic adjustment optimizes energy use, reduces wear, and maintains consistent air pressure.
